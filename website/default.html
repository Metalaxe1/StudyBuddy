The Study Buddy:

The Study Buddy is an Android chat application which gives students the ability to discuss classroom lectures and compare class notes in order to achieve higher grades through communication.

With this powerful communication tool, classmates will be able to participate in group discussions with other members of the class or communicate with another student privately.

Why Use The Study Buddy?

Simply because you know you need it!! Why would any student want to be left out?

Features:

  • Connect with classmates.

  • Share thoughts and comments.

  • Share images of notes.

  • Secure encryption

Requirements:

In order to use this software, an Android device running OS 4.4 Kit Kat or greater is required.
